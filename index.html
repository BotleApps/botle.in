About Botle

Botle is an open source initiative born from a simple belief: great tools should be free and accessible to everyone. We build apps and extensions that solve everyday problems, and we do it with passion and attention to detail.

Every line of code is written with care, every feature is crafted with the user in mind, and everything we create is open for you to use, modify, and learn from. Because we believe in the power of open source and the strength of community.
